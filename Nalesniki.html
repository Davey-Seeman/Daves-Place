Nalesniki (Polish Pancakes)

By Mary - Poland

Origins: Nalesniki is a dish home to Poland that is eaten in variety of ways both sweet and savory.
Source: https://www.tasteatlas.com/

  • Recipe:
    • Mix eggs, flour, milk, sugar, salt, and carbonated water in a bowl.
    • Let mixture rest for 20 minutes.
    • Fry mixture in butter. When the batter starts to crisp on one side (1-2 mins) flip it over. Once this happens on both sides, unload the nalesniki onto a plate.
    • Serve warm with jam (optional)

    Time to Cook:

    35 mins

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup flour
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup carbonated water
    • A pinch of salt
    • 1 tbs sugar
    • 2 tbs melted butter
    • Jam
