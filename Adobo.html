Chicken Adobo

By Carmen - Philippines

Origins: Chicken Adobo, being an iconic food from the Philippines, originates from the Spanish Word adobar which means Pickling Sauce.
Source: https://www.tasteatlas.com/

  • Recipe:
    • Chop 3 cloves of garlic, 1 onion and 3 tbsp of ginger.
    • Add 2 tbsp of oil into a large pot.
    • Put chopped garlic, onion, and ginger into the pot with the oil.
    • Fry these ingredients together until the onion becomes translucent.
    • Chop 1 chicken into quarters and put it into the pot.
    • Wait for chicken to brown.
    • Add 3 tbsp of soya sauce and 1 tsp of vinegar into the pot.
    • Cover the pot. Simmer the ingredients on low heat for approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes.

    Time to Cook:

    110 Mins

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 full chicken
    • 3 cloves of garlic
    • 3 tbsp of ginger
    • 1 tsp of vinegar
    • 1 full onion
    • 3 tbsp of soya sauce
    • 2 tbsp of oil
