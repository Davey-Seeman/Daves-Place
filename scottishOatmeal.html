Scottish Oatmeal

By Sylvia - Scotland

Origins: Scottish Oatmeal was cherished by the Scotish highlanders who lived in Soctland hundreds of years ago.
Source: Sylvia

  • Recipe:
    • Boil the water and salt in a pot.
    • After water is boiling, add the oats into the pot.
    • Cook the mixture for 7 minutes. While cooking, stir the mixture.
    • Cool for 1 minute.
    • Spoon into a bowl, and top with strawberries and maple syrup.

    Time to Cook:

    12 Mins

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup Scottish Steel
      cut quick cook oats
    • 2 cups water
    • Pinch of salt
    • Strawberries (Optional)
    • Maple Syrup (Optional)
